SMS Coaching Program

Leadership Lab uses SMS to deliver daily coaching prompts, session reminders, and reflection exercises to enrolled program participants.

How Enrollment Works

Participants are enrolled in Leadership Lab by their organization or directly through LeaderReps. During enrollment, each participant provides their mobile phone number and explicitly agrees to receive SMS messages from the Leadership Lab coaching program as a condition of participation. Enrollment confirms consent to receive program-related text messages from (888) 688-3045.

What You'll Receive

  • Daily leadership coaching prompts (1–2 messages per day)
  • Session reminders and scheduling notifications
  • Weekly reflection exercises and practice assignments
  • Program updates and milestone acknowledgements

Consent language presented at enrollment:

"By providing your mobile number and enrolling in Leadership Lab, you agree to receive SMS text messages from LeaderReps at the number provided, including daily coaching prompts, session reminders, and program updates. Message frequency: 1–2 messages per day. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance."

To stop receiving messages: Reply STOP to any message from (888) 688-3045 at any time. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Need help? Reply HELP or email support@leaderreps.com.
