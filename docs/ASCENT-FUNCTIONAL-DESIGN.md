# Ascent: Functional Design Document

**Purpose:** User experience specification for LeaderReps Ascent — the post-Foundation leadership operating system  
**Audience:** Product stakeholders, leadership team  
**Created:** March 16, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

**The Problem:** Foundation is a transformative 8-week experience — but what happens on Week 9? Without a clear next step, leaders lose momentum, habits fade, and organizations lose their training investment.

**The Solution:** Ascent turns a one-time training into an ongoing leadership operating system. It gives graduates:
- **Direction** — Focused 6-8 week "sprints" targeting specific skills
- **Accountability** — Monthly feedback from their actual team members
- **Proof** — Visible evidence that their leadership is improving over time
- **Challenge** — Progressively harder scenarios that keep growth interesting

**Why Leaders Stay Engaged:** Ascent leverages four psychological hooks that keep leaders coming back month after month:

| Hook | What It Does | Why It Works |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| **External Validation** | Team members rate the leader monthly | "My team noticed!" is more powerful than any badge |
| **Identity Investment** | Leaders publicly commit to focus areas | Once you say "I'm working on feedback," you work on it |
| **Unlockable Challenges** | New skills unlock only after proving mastery | Gamification done right — earned, not given |
| **Visible Progress** | Trend lines show improvement over time | Seeing the line go up is addictive |
