## Production Notes

### For 2-Minute Explainer
- **Style:** Clean, professional, app demo footage mixed with lifestyle B-roll
- **Music:** Upbeat but not cheesy, modern corporate
- **Graphics:** Stats appear as animated text overlays
- **Voiceover:** Warm, confident, conversational (not salesy)

### For Social Clips
- **Style:** Vertical (9:16), captions burned in, quick cuts
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds must grab attention
- **Text:** Large, readable, high contrast
- **CTA:** Always end with clear next step
