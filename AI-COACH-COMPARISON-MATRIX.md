## Decision Points

### 1. One Platform or Multiple Apps?
**Question:** Should we consolidate or maintain separate experiences?

| Option | Description | Pros | Cons |
|--------|-------------|------|------|
| **Ascent only** | Enhance existing Arena dashboard | Single platform, simpler | Limited AI conversation |
| **Ascent + Reppy** | Keep both as-is | Flexibility, different modes | Fragmentation, two apps to maintain |
| **Merge into Ascent** | Add Reppy's AI to Arena | Unified experience | More complex platform |
| **New unified app** | Build Crissy or Jeffy fresh | Clean slate, coherent vision | Abandons existing work |

### 2. Engagement Philosophy
**Question:** Do leaders need prompts/carrots to engage, or does that undermine development?

| If you believe... | Then lean toward... |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Adults under pressure need reminders | Ascent, Reppy, or Jeffy |
| Carrots create false engagement | Crissy |
| It depends on the leader's stage | Adaptive model (start with structure, reduce over time) |

### 3. AI's Role
**Question:** What should the AI actually do?

| If you believe... | Then lean toward... |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| AI should deliver content at the right time | Ascent |
| AI should be a conversational coach | Reppy |
| AI should detect patterns and surface insights | Crissy |
| AI should help with practical tasks | Jeffy |

### 4. Utility vs. Development
**Question:** Is the primary value practical help or capability growth?

| If you believe... | Then lean toward... |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Help me prepare for my 1:1 | Jeffy |
| Help me see how I'm growing | Crissy |
| Help me build daily habits | Reppy |
| Help me consume great content | Ascent |

### 5. Tracking & Visibility
**Question:** Should progress be visually tracked or felt through evidence?

| If you believe... | Then lean toward... |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Show me charts, challenges completed | Ascent or Jeffy |
| Reflect my patterns back to me | Crissy |
| Show me session completion and streaks | Reppy |

### 6. Content Strategy
**Question:** How does the Read & Reps library and other content fit?

| If you believe... | Then lean toward... |
|-------------------|---------------------|
