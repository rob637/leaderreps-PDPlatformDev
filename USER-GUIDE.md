## 2. Your Daily Practice

### The Power of Reps

Leadership is like fitness - it requires consistent practice. We call these small daily actions **"Reps"**. Each rep builds your leadership muscle over time.

### Morning Routine (AM Bookend)

Start your day with intention:

#### 1. Grounding Rep
- Read your **Leadership Identity Statement**
- Reflect on who you want to be as a leader
- Tap "I'm Grounded" when complete

#### 2. Win the Day
- Set **3 high-impact priorities** for today
- Focus on what will move the needle most
- These become your daily wins to track

#### 3. Daily Reps
- Review your assigned leadership habits
- Commit to practicing them today
- Check them off as you complete them

### Evening Routine (PM Bookend)

End your day with reflection:

#### 1. Review Your Wins
- Mark each win as ‚úÖ Complete or ‚è≠Ô∏è Carry Over
- Incomplete wins automatically carry to tomorrow

#### 2. Daily Reflection
Answer three questions:
- **What went well?** - Celebrate your successes
- **What could be better?** - Learn from challenges
- **What will you focus on?** - Set tomorrow's intention

#### 3. Check Your Scorecard
- See your daily completion percentage
- Grounding + Wins + Reps = Your daily score

### Understanding Your Streak üî•

Your streak counts consecutive days of activity:

| What You Did | Streak Result |
|--------------|---------------|
| Completed grounding, win, or rep | +1 day |
| Weekend with no activity | Maintained (grace period) |
| Weekday with no activity | Reset to 0 |

**Tip:** Even completing just your Grounding Rep keeps your streak alive!

### Leadership Identity Statement (LIS) Maker

Craft your personal Leadership Identity Statement:
1. Access the LIS Maker card on the dashboard.
2. Follow the prompt: "I am a [Core Value] leader who [Action] to create [Impact]."
3. Use the text area to draft and refine your statement.
4. Your statement will be saved and used in your daily grounding rep.

## 9. Tips for Success

### Building the Habit

1. **Start small** - Even 5 minutes counts
2. **Same time daily** - Consistency builds habits
3. **Stack habits** - Pair with existing routines (coffee, commute)
4. **Use reminders** - Set notification alerts

### Maximizing Your Development

1. **Complete content weekly** - Don't fall behind
2. **Engage with community** - Learn from peers
3. **Apply daily reps** - Practice makes permanent
4. **Reflect honestly** - Growth comes from awareness

### Streak Strategies

1. **Never miss twice** - One miss is okay, two breaks momentum
2. **Minimum viable action** - At least do grounding rep
3. **Weekday focus** - Weekends have grace period
4. **Check in early** - Morning completion sets the tone

### Getting Unstuck

If you're struggling:
1. **Simplify** - Focus on just one activity
2. **Connect** - Reach out in community
3. **Ask** - Use coaching office hours
4. **Review** - Read your past reflections for insights

## 10. FAQ & Troubleshooting

### Common Questions

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
> Your streak will reset on weekdays, but incomplete wins carry over automatically. Just jump back in tomorrow!

**Q: Can I edit past entries?**
> Current day entries can be edited. Past entries are archived and read-only to preserve your authentic history.

**Q: How do I unlock more content?**
> Content unlocks automatically as you progress through your Development Plan weeks. Premium content requires a premium membership.

**Q: Can I restart my Development Plan?**
> Contact support to discuss restarting. We can reset your plan while preserving your practice history.

**Q: Why can't I see certain content?**
> Content may be locked because:
> - It's for a future week (keep progressing!)
> - It requires premium membership
> - It's not yet assigned to your plan

### Technical Issues

**App won't load:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl/Cmd + R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection

**Notifications not working:**
1. Check browser notification permissions
2. Ensure notifications are enabled in app settings
3. Try toggling off and on again
4. Check system notification settings

**Data not saving:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Try logging out and back in
4. Clear browser cache

**Login issues:**
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Use "Forgot Password" if needed
3. Try Google sign-in as alternative
4. Contact support if persists

### Getting Help

- **In-App:** Use the help icon (?) for contextual help
- **Community:** Post questions in the forum
- **Coaching:** Attend office hours for guidance
- **Support:** Email support@leaderreps.com
